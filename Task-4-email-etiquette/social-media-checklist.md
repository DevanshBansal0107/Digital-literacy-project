Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

As a college student, using social media responsibly is important for maintaining a positive digital presence and reputation.

# Do's

*   Think before you post anything online
*   Keep your profile professional and organised with appropriate content
*   Use privacy settings to protect your personal information
*   Communicate respectfully and be mindful of others’ opinions
*   Check and verify information before sharing it with others

# Don'ts

*   Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
*   Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content that may harm your image
*   Do not spread rumours or unverified information online
*   Avoid using abusive language or getting into unnecessary arguments
*   Do not post in haste without thinking about the consequences

# Conclusion

Being responsible on social media helps you build a positive image, stay safe online, and avoid misunderstandings or conflicts.
